Shield

A stalwart defensive unit, competent in combat and extremely effective at blocking ranged attacks.

  • A single handed sword strike.
  • A stunning shield bash.
  • A defensive stance capable of protecting nearby units.

Warrior

An offensive powerhouse, capable of smashing through shields and slashing multiple targets.

  • A two handed strike.
  • A staggering horizontal sweep, capable of felling multiple targets at once.
  • A powerful overhead strike, capable of cleaving through shields and delivering tremendous damage.

Archer

A long range unit, extremely effective when kept at a safe range.

  • A long range direct arrow shot.
  • A high-arching arrow that can reach units behind obstacles.
  • A desperate melee attack usable at close range.

Rogue

A highly mobile and versatile unit, best used as a supplementary force.

  • A quick slice with dual blades.
  • A short range thrown dagger.
  • A precise stab that's especially lethal when executed from behind.
